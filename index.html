The Proving Ground

Next-Level Product Sprint 2026 is a premier global hackathon engineered for builders, innovators, and visionaries who refuse to stop at the prototype stage.

We live in a world where technology is evolving faster than the systems built to support it. The gap between a clever idea and a production-grade solution has never been more consequential — and that's exactly the gap we're challenging you to close.

Every submission will be evaluated on technical depth, architectural soundness, innovation, scalability, and real-world deployability. We push teams to go beyond polished demos and build solutions that could genuinely ship, scale, and survive in production.

// evaluation rubric

Judging Criteria

Every submission is evaluated across five dimensions by domain-expert judges.

25%
Innovation & Creativity
How original is the solution? Does it demonstrate a new approach or unique idea?
25%
Technical Complexity & Execution
Is the implementation robust, functional, and scalable?
20%
Impact & Usefulness
Does the solution solve a real-world problem or add genuine value?
15%
User Experience & Design
Is the product intuitive, easy to use, and visually appealing?
15%
Presentation & Demo
How clearly is the idea communicated? Is the demo compelling?
Submission Checklist
✅ Working Demo (video or live)
✅ Public GitHub Repository
✅ README + Architecture Docs
✅ Slide Deck / One-Pager
✅ Team Info + LinkedIn Profiles
